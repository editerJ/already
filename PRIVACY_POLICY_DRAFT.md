# Already Privacy Policy Draft

Last updated: 2026-04-30

Already is a journaling and self-reflection app. The app stores journal content locally on the user's device by default.

## Data the app processes

- Journal entries written by the user
- Daily mission and reminder state
- Language preference and local reward/pass state
- Advertising consent state when ads are enabled

## Where data is stored

- Core app content is stored locally on-device
- No account is required to use the app
- No cloud sync is enabled by default in the current release build

## Advertising

- The app may display rewarded ads through Google AdMob
- In regions where consent is required, the app uses Google's User Messaging Platform (UMP) to collect and manage ad consent

## Data sharing

- The developer does not intentionally sell journal content
- AdMob and related Google mobile ads services may process device and advertising data according to their own policies

## User controls

- Users can uninstall the app to remove the local app container from the device
- Users can reset local reward/coaching state from inside the app when that control is exposed
